Data Center Interview Guide (中英文对照)

1. 常见面试问题 / Common Interview Questions

自我介绍

Tell me about your background and experience in data centers.

技术经验

What experience do you have with rack and stack? How do you handle fiber optic cables? What’s the difference between single-mode and multi-mode fiber? Can you explain the difference between SFP, SFP+, and QSFP? Describe your experience with ticketing systems like ServiceNow. Have you worked with Excel? Can you use pivot tables or formulas?

场景类问题

A server is not powering on after installation, how would you troubleshoot? You receive multiple urgent tickets at the same time, how do you prioritize? If a cable is mislabeled, what steps do you take to fix it?

软技能 / 团队

Are you comfortable working night shifts and weekends? Can you lift heavy equipment (servers, UPS, etc.)? Do you have leadership experience in a data center environment?

3. 英文回答示例 / Example Answers

Q: Tell me about your data center experience.

👉 “I have over five years of experience working in data centers. My responsibilities included racking and stacking servers, running fiber and Ethernet cables, and managing equipment inventory. I also used ServiceNow for ticket management and Excel for tracking assets.”

Q: What’s the difference between single-mode and multi-mode fiber?

👉 “Single-mode fiber is designed for long-distance communication using a laser, usually with yellow jackets. Multi-mode fiber is used for shorter distances, typically inside data centers, and uses LED light, often with orange or aqua jackets.”

Q: How do you troubleshoot a server that won’t power on?

👉 “First, I would check the power connections and ensure redundant power supplies are connected. Then I would verify if the PDU is providing power. If hardware seems fine, I would check logs or iDRAC/ILO for errors. If needed, I’d escalate through the ticketing system.”

Q: Are you comfortable working night shifts?

👉 “Yes, I understand this role requires evening and weekend shifts. I am flexible and used to working in a 24/7 environment.”

4. Rack & Stack 常用单词汇总 / Vocabulary

Rack → 机柜

Rack Unit (U) → 机柜高度单位（1U=1.75 英寸）

Rails → 导轨（安装服务器用的金属滑轨）

Blanking Panel / Filler Panel → 挡板（填充空位，保证气流）

Cable Management Arm (CMA) → 线缆管理臂（服务器后方，方便走线）

Rack Mount Kit → 上架套件（螺丝、导轨、支架）

电源相关 PDU / PSU / Power Cord / Redundant Power Supply

网络 & 线缆 Patch Panel / Ethernet Cable / Fiber Optic Cable / SFP / SFP+ / QSFP / Transceiver / Cable Label

服务器安装相关 Rack & Stack / Unboxing / Mounting Bracket / Thumbscrews / Cage Nuts / Grounding

散热 & 环境 Airflow / Hot Aisle / Cold Aisle / Cooling Fan / Temperature Sensor

举例: “When racking and stacking a server, how do you ensure proper airflow?”

👉 “I always install blanking panels in unused rack spaces and make sure cables are properly managed to avoid blocking airflow.”

5. 场景练习 / Scenario Practice

Supervisor: “Hey, we need to rack and stack this new Dell server in Rack 12. Can you take care of it?”

You: “Sure. I’ll start by unboxing the server and checking the rail kit.”

Supervisor: “Good. Make sure you install the rails properly. Do you need any help?”

You: “I think I’m good. I’ll mount the rails, slide the server into place, and secure it with cage nuts and screws.”

Supervisor: “Don’t forget about power and network connections.”

You: “Understood. I’ll connect both redundant PSUs to separate PDUs for power redundancy, and then connect the Ethernet and fiber cables. I’ll also label all the cables.”

Supervisor: “Nice. How about airflow management?”

You: “I’ll install blanking panels in empty rack spaces and make sure the cable management arm doesn’t block the airflow.”

Supervisor: “Great. Once it’s powered on, log a ticket in ServiceNow to confirm installation.”

You: “Will do. I’ll test the server power, check the iDRAC interface, and then update the ServiceNow ticket with the installation details.”
